# Phase 90-12: AD Emulator Role Awareness

## Objective

Enhance the AD Emulator to track and sync employee role types (Department Head vs Regular Employee), enabling RiskHub to make smarter assignment decisions and enforce ownership hierarchies.

## Execution Context

@AD Emulator/backend/app/models/directory_user.py
@AD Emulator/backend/app/schemas/directory_user.py
@AD Emulator/frontend/src/components/UserForm.tsx
@backend/app/services/directory_sync_service.py
@backend/app/models/user.py

## Context

### Problem Statement
The AD Emulator currently tracks `job_title` but has no structured way to indicate whether a user is a **Department Head** or **Regular Employee**. This distinction is important for:
- Auto-assigning ownership of orphaned items to dept heads
- Permission filtering (dept heads may have broader access)
- Org chart visualization

### Solution
Add an `employee_type` enum field to `DirectoryUser` with values:
- `head` - Department Head/Manager
- `employee` - Regular Employee
- `contractor` - External Contractor (future)

Sync this field to RiskHub's users table and use it in orphan resolution suggestions.
