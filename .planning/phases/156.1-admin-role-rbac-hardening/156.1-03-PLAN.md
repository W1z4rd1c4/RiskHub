  <files>backend/app/schemas/admin.py, backend/app/api/v1/endpoints/admin.py</files>

Implement request/response contract rules:

Canonical fields:
- `app_log_rotation_size_mb`
- `app_log_retention_count`
- `audit_log_rotation_size_mb`

- `log_rotation_size_mb`
