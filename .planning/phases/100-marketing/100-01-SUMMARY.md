# Summary: Phase 100-01 (Marketing Presentation)

Implemented a high-end, standalone HTML presentation designed for pitching RiskHub to the board of directors.

## Accomplishments
- **Premium Design**: Created a single-file presentation with glassmorphism, mesh gradients, and smooth transitions (no external dependencies required).
- **Comprehensive Content**: Covered every major feature of the platform, from Risk Registry and Control Catalog to KRI monitoring and AD integration.
- **Czech Localization**: All content is written in professional, corporate-level Czech, specifically using technical terminology from OS 18 (CNB).
- **Interactive Navigation**: Integrated a keyboard, touch, and button-based slide controller using Vanilla JavaScript.

## Functions Presented
1.  **Registr rizik**: Gross/Net scoring, heatmaps, S/O categories.
2.  **Katalog kontrol**: 13-point structure, risk linkage, efficiency evaluation.
3.  **KRI Monitoring**: Limit tracking, semaphore signaling, breach detection.
4.  **Dashboards**: Executive and Department views with interactive drill-downs.
5.  **Governance & AD**: Real-time AD sync and Orphaned item management.
6.  **Reporting & Audit**: Excel/PDF exports and full field-level historization.
7.  **Workflow & Security**: Multi-level approvals and role-based access control.

- **Layout**: Verified responsive design and premium typography.
