```markdown
### 5.6 Approval Permission System

#### Who Can Approve/Reject Approval Requests

The ability to approve or reject requests requires **TWO conditions**:

| Condition | Check | Description |
|-----------|-------|-------------|
| **Privileged User** | `access_scope = GLOBAL` | C-Suite or Governance roles |
| **Permission** | `approvals:write` or `*:*` | Explicit permission grant |

**Roles with approval capability by default:**
- CRO (via `*:*` wildcard)
- Admin (via `*:*` wildcard)
- Risk Manager (via `approvals:write`)
- Compliance (via `approvals:write`)
- Legal (via `approvals:write`)
- Internal Audit (via `approvals:write`)
- Actuarial (via `approvals:write`)

#### Risk Hub Configuration vs RBAC

The Risk Hub "Approval Rules" tab configures **notification routing**—which roles 
receive notifications about pending approvals. The actual ability to approve 
depends on RBAC permissions.

| System | Purpose | Location |
|--------|---------|----------|
| `approval_scenarios.approver_roles` | Notification routing | Risk Hub UI |
| `role_permissions` + `approvals:write` | Approval capability | Database RBAC |

> [!IMPORTANT]
> If CRO adds a role to "approvers" in Risk Hub, that role must also have 
> `approvals:write` permission or `*:*` wildcard to actually approve requests.
```
