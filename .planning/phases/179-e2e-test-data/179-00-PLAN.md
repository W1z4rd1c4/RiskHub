> **Phase Goal**: Create comprehensive insurance risk data with cross-department ownership to enable E2E tests that currently skip due to missing data.

---

## Problem Statement

## Risk Warnings

> [!CAUTION]
> **Risk #1: Data Pollution**  
> Seeded data may conflict with migrated Excel data. **Mitigation**: All entities use `E2E-` prefix.

> [!CAUTION]
> **Risk #2: FK Dependencies**  
> Scripts depend on existing demo users. **Mitigation**: Plan 179-01 validates prerequisites.

> [!WARNING]
> **Risk #3: Approval State Drift**  
> Pre-seeded approvals may be resolved during dev. **Mitigation**: Document which approvals are test fixtures.

> [!WARNING]
> **Risk #4: Activity Log Pollution**  
> Seeding creates audit entries. **Mitigation**: Use recognizable prefixes in descriptions.
