## Baseline Before Execution

This overview originally captured pre-execution conditions where large parts of E2E coverage were skip-driven due to missing deterministic data. The execution phase expanded from the initial 6-plan baseline to a full 17-plan set (`179-00` through `179-16`).

## Constraints Applied

- No creation of new risk types.
- No creation of new users/departments by E2E scripts.
- Use existing demo users from `app/db/seed.py`.
- Keep deterministic `E2E-` fixture naming and idempotent rerun behavior.
