<task type="auto">
  <name>Task 1: Seed parity — model “legal” as permission capability</name>
  <action>
In many orgs, legal responsibilities are handled by the Compliance Officer role. For RiskHub Phase 18 we model that as a capability permission rather than a separate role.

Update `backend/app/db/seed.py`:
- Add `vendor_contracts:read` and `vendor_contracts:write` to PERMISSIONS.
- Assign `vendor_contracts:*` to `compliance` by default (and optionally `cro` / `risk_manager` if desired).
- Do NOT add a separate `legal` role for Phase 18.

Do not attempt to “perfectly” rewrite the full permission matrix here—keep changes minimal and focused on enabling Phase 18 testing and avoiding role-not-found inconsistencies.
  </action>
  <done>Local/dev seed supports Phase 18 contract governance via `vendor_contracts:*` permissions without requiring a separate `legal` role.</done>
</task>
