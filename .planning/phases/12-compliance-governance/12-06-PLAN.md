## Technical Details
Enterprise security teams often want to ingest *only* the Audit Trail, not every debugging `print` statement. We will split the stream.

Target Architecture:
- `logs/app.json.log`: General application debug/info (rotated daily, keep 7 days).
- `logs/audit.json.log`: High-value Audit/Security events (rotated by size 50MB, keep 3 years archived or forwarded).
