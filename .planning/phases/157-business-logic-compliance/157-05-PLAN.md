## Objective

Align the role naming in `seed.py` with BUSINESS_LOGIC.md §1.1 and ensure consistency with `seed_demo.py`.

## Context

**BUSINESS_LOGIC.md §1.1** lists these roles:

- ceo, cfo, cro, coo
- risk_manager, compliance, legal, internal_audit, actuarial
- department_head, **employee**
- admin, viewer

**Issue in `seed.py`:**

- Uses `control_owner` role (lines 22, 60, 82-89) instead of `employee`

**`seed_demo.py`** correctly uses `employee` role.
