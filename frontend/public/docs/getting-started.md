# Getting Started with RiskHub

Welcome to RiskHub, your enterprise risk management platform.

## First Steps

1. **Dashboard** — Your home screen shows an overview of organizational risk health
2. **Navigation** — Use the sidebar to access Risks, Controls, KRIs, and more
3. **Your Profile** — Check Settings → Profile to see your role and permissions

## Need Help?

Contact your organization's Risk Management team or IT support for assistance.
