# Department Head Guide

## Your Responsibilities

As a Department Head, you can:
- Manage risks within your department
- Oversee control execution
- Review KRI submissions from your team
- Approve changes within your scope

## Team Overview

Navigate to **Departments** to see your team's risk posture.

## Approval Workflow

You may receive approval requests from team members. Check the **Workflow** page for pending items.
