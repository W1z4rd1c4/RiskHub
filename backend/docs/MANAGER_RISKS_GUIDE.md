# Managing Risks

## Viewing Risks

Navigate to **Risks** in the sidebar to see all risks you have access to.

## Risk Details

Each risk includes:
- **Name** and **Description** 
- **Gross Score** — Risk before controls
- **Net Score** — Risk after controls
- **Owner** — Person responsible
- **Department** — Business area

## Creating Risks

If you have write permissions, click **+ Add Risk** to create a new risk.
